Power Apps

Power Apps is a low-code development platform offered by Microsoft that allows users to build custom business applications without extensive coding knowledge. With Power Apps, individuals and teams can create web and mobile applications that solve specific business problems or automate manual processes.

The platform provides a visual interface and a range of pre-built templates and components, making it easy for users to design and develop apps using a drag-and-drop approach. Power Apps supports various data sources, including Microsoft 365, SharePoint, Dynamics 365, and external systems, allowing users to connect to and leverage existing data.

Power Apps offers two types of apps: canvas apps and model-driven apps. Canvas apps provide complete design flexibility, enabling users to create highly customized user interfaces and workflows. Model-driven apps, on the other hand, are built on a data model and provide a structured approach to app development, with predefined layouts and components.

With Power Apps, users can create apps for various purposes, such as data entry forms, approval workflows, inventory tracking, employee onboarding, and customer engagement. These apps can be accessed on mobile devices, web browsers, or integrated directly into other Microsoft platforms like Teams and SharePoint.
